Frequently asked questions

How do you charge — hourly or flat rate?

It depends on the job. Smaller tasks and odd-job lists are usually billed hourly, while bigger or well-defined projects get a flat quote so you know the cost up front.

Is there a minimum job size?

We have a small minimum to make the trip worthwhile, but we're happy to knock out several small fixes in one visit so you get the most value for it.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We're fully insured for your peace of mind, and we'll happily share details when we come out to quote your job.

What areas do you serve?

We cover Acworth, Kennesaw, Marietta, Smyrna, and the surrounding Cobb and Fulton County areas across Metro Atlanta. Not sure if you're in range? Just ask.

How soon can you come out?

We can usually schedule within a few days, and we do our best to fit in urgent fixes sooner. Call or text and we'll find a time that works.

Do you guarantee your work?

Absolutely. If something isn't right, let us know and we'll come back to make it right — no hassle. We're not finished until it's done right.
