## What to do next (to complete the missing emails quickly)

## 1) 2-hour inside-sales sprint
For each “No public email found” company:
1. Call main office.
2. Ask for: estimating inbox, purchasing inbox, and accounts-payable inbox.
3. Confirm preferred bid-submission email and accepted file format.
4. Add contact owner name + title to CRM.

## 2) Standard call script
“Hi, we supply commercial insulation across NYC/LI. Who is the best email for bid requests and day-to-day material orders? Also, do you use a separate AP email for new vendor forms?”

## 3) CRM fields to capture immediately
- estimator_email
- purchasing_email
- ap_email
- contact_name
- contact_title
- product_focus (mechanical / firestop / thermal / acoustical)
- avg_order_frequency
